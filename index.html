Our Mission

Innovation

"Drive the development of unmanned aerial systems through structural design, mission execution, and spatial data processing. We transform academic science into applicable solutions for the agricultural and territorial sector.".

Our group brings together doctors, engineers, and students committed to scientific advancement and the development of cutting-edge technology for Ecuador and the region.

Remote Monitoring

Crop study including potato, onion, and pitahaya

Vehicle Design

Assembly of composite materials for simulation and design.

Mission Deployment

Flight planning for field reconnaissance.

Data Processing

Map creation, digital surface models with terrain elevation.
